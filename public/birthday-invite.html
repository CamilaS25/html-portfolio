What would you find in this app?

  • Spaces Options:
    • If you're looking for a way to reorganize your work or integrate your colleagues's work in 1 space, you could use the WORK option.
    • If you want to track your goals, tasks and more for personal purposes only, you can use the PERSONAL space.
    • You can also find spaces to track your Training, study, and more!
  • Themes and layouts: you can customize the appareance of your Spaces to match with your needs and style. Make this space more flashy and less boring. You will spend some time here, so enjoy it!
  • Features: you can create Checklists or To Do lists, tables with columns such as tasks, dates, priority, status, tags, drop-down, and more! Additionally, you can choose if you want to see your tasks in a calendar, tables, or board. You can track your reports and goals, etc.
